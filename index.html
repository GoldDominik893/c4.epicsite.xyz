Description

Transparent process

This battery pack is a DIY project built from three LiPo cells arranged in parallel, encased in a hot-glue shell for added puncture resistance. It features dual XT60 connectors for versatile connectivity and has been tested to handle peak currents of up to 100A, however the battery will heat up and may cause a fire, I would only ever draw a maximum of 10A from it. The pack is designed for low power applications, such as charging a phone etc, with a total capacity of 148Wh, making it suitable for various electronics projects. Safety is a priority; the pack includes basic protective measures, but users should still follow best practices when handling and charging LiPo batteries.
